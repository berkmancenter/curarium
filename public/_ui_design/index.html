Collections
Curarium leverages the power of the crowd to annotate, curate, & augment works with the aim of constructing shareable, media-rich stories & elaborate arguments about individual items as well as groups of items. Collections: Find and follow collections of interest to you OR ingest your own collection* of up to 30,000 records in just 5 minutes. Spotlights: Tell stories by pulling directly from the content you’ve collected in your trays. Publish, share, and export your spotlights. Explore: Within any collection, browse, filter, search, and generate visualizations. Annotate records and make additions to their metadata. Save individual records, groups of records, or visualizations to a tray. Categorize what you save using as many trays as you need. You: Start or join user circles to share and collaborate on work within a specified collection or set of collections. When you explore any collections in your circles, you can use a social tray to work in real time with circle members to collect and order records.
